Running

Running is an excellent way to clear your head or catch up on your favorite TV shows. Whether on a treadmill with a screen for entertainment or outdoors in a park to enjoy nature, running offers a range of enjoyment. Each environment has its own appeal, indoor running allows for catching up on your favorite shows, while outdoor running lets you immerse yourself in nature.

Lifting

Lifting, a necessity and usually a great time to read, explore new music, or dive into podcasts. Some of my favorite genres to listen to during this time are hard rock and hip-hop. As for podcasts, I often enjoy Ginger Whiskey or Lenny's Podcast, depending on what I'm interested in at the moment.

TV Shows and Movies

First up is Naruto. It's not something I watch much these days, but it was on often while I was growing up, sparking my love for anime. Not all anime, of course, some of my favorites are Bleach, Fire Force, and Jujutsu Kaisen. When new episodes are released, these 25 minute blocks are a perfect way to add a bit of fun to the day. Other favorites include SEAL Team, Lincoln Lawyer, Outer Banks, and Marvel movies. These shows and movies are a great way to take a break in a busy schedule.
